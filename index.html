About

    Prof. Maithili Andhare is an Assistant Professor in the Department of Electronics and Telecommunication Engineering at Pimpri Chinchwad College of Engineering & Research, Pune. With over 18 years of teaching experience, she is dedicated to mentoring students, fostering innovation, and advancing research in cutting-edge technologies. She holds a B.E. in Electronics Engineering from SRT University, Nanded, and an M.E. in Electronics Engineering from SPPU, Pune. Currently, she is pursuing a Ph.D. at BIHER College, Chennai, specializing in Embedded Systems and IoT.Her passion lies in bridging academia with real-world applications, driving innovation through research, and contributing to the evolving landscape of electronics and telecommunications.
